DIEGO ARMANDO MARADONA

1960-2020

Biography

Diego Armando Maradona was an argentine professional football player and manager born on 30th October 1960 at Lanus,Agentina and died on the 25th November 2020 in his home country.His vision, passing, ball control, and dribbling skills combined with his low small stature allowed him to manoeuvre his way arounf the pitch better than most players.Being a free-kick specialist among his other skills and talent earned him the nickname "El Pibe de Oro" meaning the golden boy,a name that stuck with him throughout his career.He played at Argentinos Juniors during his junior career and teams like napoli, barcelona during his senior career.Diego Maradona is arguably one of the best football players of all time.
